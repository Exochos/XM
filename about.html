We are committed to bringing Web3 to the public - enriching our investors - and reducing carbon

E-commerce, Delivery and Ride-hailing businesses are rapidly growing. Currently, these businesses rely almost entirely on internal-combustion engines throughout the delivery chain, resulting in significant increases in pollution, greenhouse gasses, and traffic congestion. In 2021, human activities pumped 36 billion tons of planet-warming gas into the atmosphere – the highest in human history. Considering anticipated demand, shippers and third-party delivery companies must adopt new best practices that electrify their supply chain, lowering greenhouse gasses without compromising speed or reliability, as measured by the count of Parcel Delivered to the Customers Successfully (PDCS).

CO2 is a harmful, generally human-induced, greenhouse gas that stays in the atmosphere for hundreds to thousands of years – almost 90% of all CO2 emissions come from burning fossil fuels. Of this total, transportation accounts for around one-fifth (20%) of the emissions. Of this total, approximately 40% were caused by pollution from “last-mile” deliveries.

Zero-emission commercial electric vehicles must be our priority.
